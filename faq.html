Money

How much does it cost?
$1,400/month for management — everything we do: ads, creative, your booking page, automated follow-up, dashboard, and support. Your first 30 days of management are free. Separately, you fund your own ad budget: minimum $50/day (~$1,500/month), paid directly to Meta on your own ad account — we never touch it or mark it up. All-in after the free month, plan on roughly $2,900–$3,500/month. One sold treatment package typically covers it.
Why do I pay for ads separately?
Transparency. Your ad dollars go straight from your card to Meta on an ad account you own — you can see every penny in Ads Manager. Our fee only pays for the system and the people running it. Agencies that "include" ad spend are marking it up; we'd rather you see the real numbers.
How do payments work?
Bank autopay (ACH) — you connect your business bank account once at signup (about 90 seconds, no voided checks), and the monthly fee runs automatically. Cancel anytime, effective at the end of the paid month.
Is there a contract?
No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. We keep clients with results, not paperwork.
Do you guarantee results?
No — and be wary of anyone who does. Every figure we show (including the calculator on our homepage) is an estimate based on typical performance across shockwave clinics using this system. Your results depend on your market, budget, offer, and how well your consult converts. What we can promise: proven creative, instant lead follow-up, honest reporting, and no lock-in if it's not working.

Getting started

How does the free 30 days work?
Your first month of management is completely free — you only fund your ad budget. Full timeline on the Free Trial page. At day 30 you look at a month of real numbers and decide. No exit fees either way.
How fast will I be live?
3–5 business days from finishing the onboarding form. Most of that is phone-carrier approval for the automated texting (a compliance step every business goes through). The ads themselves are built in 2–3 days.
What do I actually have to do?
Three things, ever: a 15-minute onboarding (one form + connecting your Facebook ad account — our Meta setup guide makes it painless), treat the patients who book, and a monthly 15-minute check-in call on your numbers. No filming, no writing, no ad management.
I've never run Facebook ads — do I need to set something up?
Yes, once: a free Meta ad account that you own (about 10 minutes), so your ad dollars go straight from your card to Meta with full transparency. Follow the step-by-step setup guide — it ends with one secure click that connects our team to run everything for you.
I don't own a Pulse Wave machine — can I still join?
Yes. The program was built for our machine customers first, but it works for any clinic offering shockwave therapy (and we'll tell you honestly if your setup isn't a fit). Fill out the interest form and mention what device you run.
Do I need a website already?
No. We build you a dedicated booking page designed for shockwave patients — leads book straight onto your calendar. Your existing website (or lack of one) doesn't matter.

How the marketing works

Who actually runs my ads?
A dedicated fulfillment team that runs patient marketing for shockwave clinics nationwide — using ad creative that's already been tested across the network, so your budget skips the expensive trial-and-error phase. Your account manager is your single point of contact.
How many appointments should I expect?
Typical clinics see 10–15+ booked appointments per month at $50/day in ad spend, at a typical cost of $100–150 per booked appointment — and it scales with budget (play with the calculator). First 1–2 weeks are slower while Meta's algorithm learns your market. Estimates, not guarantees.
What about no-shows?
Normal and planned for: the network showing rate averages 60–80%. Every no-show automatically enters a text sequence that re-books them instead of losing them. A no-show isn't a lost patient — it's a delayed one.
Do the ads use my clinic's name?
The ads promote shockwave therapy for pain relief in your area, and every lead books with your clinic on your calendar. You review and approve what runs for your practice.
What's in the dashboard?
Every lead, booked appointment, show, and conversation in one place, updated live. You'll never wonder what your money did this month.
